Percentage Formulas Explained

1. Finding X% of Y

Formula: (X √∑ 100) √ó Y

Example: What is 25% of 200?

2. X is What % of Y

Formula: (X √∑ Y) √ó 100

Example: 30 is what percent of 150?
(30 √∑ 150) √ó 100 = 0.2 √ó 100 = 20%

3. Percentage Increase

Formula: X + (X √ó Y √∑ 100)

Example: Increase 80 by 25%

4. Percentage Decrease

Formula: X - (X √ó Y √∑ 100)

Example: From 50 to 75 is what % change?
