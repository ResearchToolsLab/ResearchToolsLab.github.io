Understanding Invoice Components

Essential Invoice Elements

  • Business Name: Your company or individual name ‚Äî appears as "From" on invoice
  • Client Name: Customer or client name ‚Äî appears as "Bill To"
  • Invoice Number: Unique identifier for tracking and record-keeping (e.g., #1001, #1002)
  • Invoice Date: Date of invoice generation ‚Äî important for payment terms
  • Line Items: Products/services with description, quantity, unit price, and line total
  • Subtotal: Sum of all line item totals before taxes
  • Tax: GST, VAT, or sales tax percentage applied to subtotal
  • Total Amount: Final amount due (subtotal + tax)
  • Payment Terms: Due date, payment methods, late fee policies

Why Tax Calculation Matters

Accurate GST and VAT calculations are legally required in many countries. Our invoice generator automatically computes tax amounts based on your entered percentage, ensuring compliance and avoiding calculation errors.
